About Me

Who I Am

I am an AI researcher and computer science graduate with a deep commitment to solving real-world problems through intelligent systems. My work spans medical image analysis, computer vision, bioinformatics, and deep learning — bridging the gap between theoretical research and impactful applications.

Currently, I serve as a Research Assistant in the Machine Learning Group at Qatar University, where I apply AI and machine learning in healthcare and agriculture. I'm simultaneously pursuing my MSc at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia, focusing on hyperspectral imaging with advanced deep learning architectures.

Beyond research, I am a passionate competitive programmer with 1300+ problems solved and a track record in national and international contests, a community leader in AI/ML, and an advocate for gender equity in technology.
